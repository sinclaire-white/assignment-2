Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

A standard curriculum for children aged 3 to 6 years focuses on foundational learning through play, fostering social skills, creativity, and early literacy. It emphasizes exploration, problem-solving, and developing fine and gross motor abilities.

Elementary School

For elementary school, a standard curriculum emphasizes building core skills in reading, writing, math, and science, while encouraging curiosity, teamwork, and creativity. It nurtures confidence, discipline, and a love for lifelong learning.

Middle (10-16) Years

For ages 10 to 16 years, the curriculum focuses on critical thinking, deeper subject exploration, and skill-building for future success. It supports personal growth, academic challenges, struggles and preparing for higher education or career paths.
